# Premium Polish Applied 🔥

## Design System: DARK Newspaper Aesthetic

### Color Palette
- **Background:** Deep black (#0a0a0a) with charcoal accents
- **Text:** Bright white (#fafafa) on dark paper
- **Accent:** Neon green (#39FF14) - electric, bold, unmissable
- **Borders:** Subtle dark gray (#2a2a2a)

### Textures & Effects
- **Paper grain:** Aged newspaper texture overlay (8% opacity)
- **Creases & wear:** Subtle linear gradients simulating paper folds
- **Neon glow:** Pulsing green accent with shadow effects

## Premium Interactions

### 1. Button Animations
**Hero CTA:**
- Hover: -2px translate + neon green glow (40px spread)
- Shimmer effect: White gradient sweeps across on hover (1s duration)
- Active state: Returns to base position

**Secondary Buttons:**
- Border glow on hover (neon green)
- -1px translate
- 2px border thickness

### 2. Card Hover Effects
**Lab Cards (5-phase system):**
- Icon scales 1.1x on hover
- Card lifts -1px with shadow (40px blur, 50% opacity)
- Title color shifts to accent green
- Border glows neon green
- Bottom divider animates to accent color
- Duration: 350ms cubic-bezier ease

**Stats Cards:**
- Lift -2px on hover
- Number scales 1.1x
- Label shifts to accent color
- Neon green glow (20px spread, 20% opacity)
- Duration: 350ms

### 3. Image Transitions
**B&W → Color Reveal:**
- Base: B&W image visible
- Hover: Color image opacity 0 → 1
- Duration: 350ms ease-out
- NO flash, NO harsh cuts

**Pods Grid:**
- Image scales 1.05x on hover
- Border shifts from dark gray → neon green
- Gradient overlay fades in (90% black at bottom)
- Use case label appears
- Title shifts to accent color

### 4. Navigation
- Dark glass effect: 80% background opacity
- Backdrop blur: xl (strong blur)
- Shadow: 2xl depth
- Border: Subtle bottom border

## Typography

### Links
- Hover: 12px green glow with 50% opacity
