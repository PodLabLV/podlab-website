### 4️⃣ #4: Performance Optimization (Impact: 8/10)

**Why Critical:**
- Founders are busy (won't wait for slow sites)
- Mobile traffic = 50%+
- Google ranks faster sites higher

**What to Optimize:**

**A. Video Compression**
- Austin testimonial: 226MB → 40MB (85% reduction)
- Client montage: 134MB → 30MB (78% reduction)
- Tools: Handbrake, FFmpeg, or CloudConvert

**B. GIF Optimization**
- podlab-logo-live-action.gif: 30MB → 3MB
- podlab-logo-animation.gif: 30MB → 3MB
- Tools: ezgif.com, gifsicle

**C. Image Optimization**
- Convert PNGs to WebP (50-70% smaller)
- Lazy loading (Next.js Image handles this)
- Responsive images (srcset)

**D. Code Splitting**
- Assessment page: Code-split React components
- Lazy load heavy sections

**Impact:**
- Load time: 8s → 2s (400% faster)
- Better mobile experience
- Higher SEO ranking
- Lower bounce rate
- Better user experience

**Time to Implement:** 1-2 hours

---

### 5️⃣ #5: Scroll Animations + Micro-interactions (Impact: 7/10)

**Why Critical:**
- Differentiates from competitor sites
- Modern, premium feel
- Guides attention
- More engaging

**What to Add:**

**A. Fade-In on Scroll**
- Sections fade in as they enter viewport
- Cards stagger in (100ms delay each)
- Library: Framer Motion or AOS.js

**B. Parallax Backgrounds**
- GIF backgrounds move slower than scroll
- Creates depth

**C. Hover Micro-interactions**
- Buttons: Press down on click
- Cards: Lift + tilt on hover
- Links: Underline slide-in

**D. Number Counting**
- Stats count up on scroll-into-view
- "200+ Founders" → counts from 0 to 200

**E. Progress Indicators**
- Reading progress bar on blog posts
- Assessment progress (already have this ✅)

**Impact:**
- More engaging experience
- Premium brand perception
- Longer time on site
- Better storytelling

**Time to Implement:** 3-4 hours
