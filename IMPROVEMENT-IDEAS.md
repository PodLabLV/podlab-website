# 5 Website Improvements - To Enhance

**Date:** 2026-02-26 04:03 PST  
**Goal:** Take the site from great to EXCEPTIONAL

---

## INITIAL 5 IDEAS

### 1. **Scroll-Triggered Animations**
- Fade-in effects as sections enter viewport
- Stagger animations for cards/lists
- Parallax backgrounds
- **Impact:** More engaging, modern feel

### 2. **SEO Meta Tags & Open Graph**
- Add meta descriptions to all pages
- Open Graph images for social sharing
- Twitter cards
- Structured data (JSON-LD)
- **Impact:** Better search rankings + social shares

### 3. **Testimonial Quotes Section**
- Pull-quotes from video testimonials
- Rotating carousel or grid
- Client headshots
- Before/after metrics
- **Impact:** More social proof, faster trust building

### 4. **Email Capture on Assessment**
- Collect email before showing results
- Add to CRM/email list
- Send results via email
- Follow-up sequence
- **Impact:** Lead capture, nurture opportunity

### 5. **Loading States & Performance**
- Skeleton screens while content loads
- Compress videos (360MB → <100MB)
- Lazy load images
- Progressive JPEGs
- **Impact:** Better UX, faster perceived load time
