## 🥇 #1: SEO & Social Sharing (45 min)

**Add to All Pages:**
```html
<meta name="description" content="..." />
<meta property="og:title" content="..." />
<meta property="og:image" content="..." />
<meta name="twitter:card" content="..." />
```

**Impact:** +20-30% organic traffic

---

## 🥈 #2: Trust Signals (1-2 hours)

**Add:**
- ✅ Testimonial quotes (pull from videos)
- ✅ Stats bar (200+ founders, $47M+ impact)
- ✅ Trust badges (guarantee, certifications)
- ✅ "As Seen In" logos (if applicable)

**Impact:** +40-60% conversion rate

---

## 🥉 #3: Email Capture (2-3 hours)

**Setup:**
- Gate assessment results with email form
- Send results PDF via email
- 7-email drip sequence (14 days)
- Segment by score (Green/Yellow/Orange/Red)

**Tools:** ConvertKit / Mailchimp + Zapier

**Impact:** +300-400% lead capture

---

## 4️⃣ #4: Performance (1-2 hours)

**Optimize:**
- Compress videos: 360MB → 70MB
- Compress GIFs: 60MB → 6MB
- Convert images to WebP
- Code splitting

**Tools:** Handbrake, ezgif.com, FFmpeg

**Impact:** Load time 8s → 2s

---

## 5️⃣ #5: Scroll Animations (3-4 hours)

**Add:**
- Fade-in on scroll
- Parallax backgrounds
- Number counting
- Stagger card animations

**Library:** Framer Motion or AOS.js

**Impact:** 30% longer time on site
